Who we are

The Sovereign Standard Family Office is a private investment organization founded by four visionary Brazilian families, with the objective of promoting three fundamental rights: property, freedom, and privacy.

With a decade-long trajectory, the family office, originated from ByeBnk, seeks to expand its mission through investments in strategic sectors aligned with its objectives, such as blockchain, digital privacy, fintech, and artificial intelligence.

The organization aims to create a lasting impact, developing solutions that empower individuals and prioritize property, freedom, and privacy as essential assets for the future.
